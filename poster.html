Detailed Problem Title

Aurova — AI-Powered Mental Health Platform
for Women, Youth & Individuals

Problem Statement

Mental health support in India remains inaccessible, expensive, and stigmatized — especially for women, teens, and rural communities. Existing platforms like BetterHelp cost $65–$100/month, are English-only, Western-centric, and offer no anonymity. 68% of Indian women and 74% of youth report unaddressed mental health concerns due to cost, shame, or lack of culturally relevant care. There is an urgent need for a free, anonymous, multi-language, AI-powered mental health platform built for the Indian context.

Target Users
  • Women — college students, professionals, homemakers facing burnout & identity struggles
  • Youth (13–25) — dealing with exam stress, social media anxiety, self-discovery
  • Individuals — anyone seeking stigma-free, private mental health support
  • Doctors — verified mental health professionals managing practice & patients
  • Anonymous users — those who want help without revealing identity

Proposed Solution — Key Features
Future Enhancements
🤖

Context-Trained AI Chat

AI companion trained on patient Q&A dataset with 25 hardcoded context responses. Falls back to Groq LLaMA 3.1 for general queries. Multi-language voice support via Sarvam AI.

🎤

Multi-Language TTS & STT

Text-to-Speech and Speech-to-Text in Telugu, Hindi, Tamil, English & more using Sarvam AI (bulbul:v2 + saaras:v2). Browser fallback for reliability.

🔒

Anonymous Mode

Full-featured anonymous access — no email, no identity. Users create shadow nicknames for stigma-free support. Firebase Auth + custom JWT tokens.

👩‍⚕️

Expert Booking + Analytics

Verified doctor directory with real-time slot booking, patient volume charts, feedback ratings, and satisfaction breakdowns per doctor.

📊

Mood Tracking & Reports

Daily mood logging, journal entries with sentiment analysis, wellness hub with personalized insights. MUI DataGrid for booking management.

1

Fine-Tuned LLM on Clinical Data

Train a domain-specific model on anonymized patient conversations for more accurate, empathetic responses instead of hardcoded context matches.

2

Video & Voice Consultations

Real-time WebRTC video/audio sessions between patients and verified doctors, with end-to-end encryption and recording consent.

3

Wearable Integration

Connect with smartwatches to monitor heart rate, sleep patterns, and stress indicators for proactive mental health alerts.

4

Regional Language Expansion

Add support for Bengali, Marathi, Malayalam, Gujarati, and Punjabi — covering 90%+ of India's population linguistically.

5

AI Crisis Prediction

ML-based early warning system analyzing mood trends, journal sentiment, and chat patterns to flag at-risk users before crisis situations.

6

Corporate Wellness Programs

B2B tier for companies to provide employees with anonymous mental health support, aggregated analytics, and wellness workshops.
